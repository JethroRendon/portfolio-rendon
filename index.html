About Me

Hello! I’m Jethro Rendon, currently serving as an SK Chairman while pursuing my studies in Computer Science. My journey has allowed me to combine leadership in community service with my passion for technology and innovation.

As an SK Chairman, I’ve led youth-focused projects, organized community programs, and worked on initiatives that build leadership and teamwork among young people. This role has strengthened my skills in communication, project management, and decision-making.

At the same time, my path as a Computer Science student fuels my interest in solving problems through programming, web development, and software design. I’m passionate about building digital solutions that can make processes easier, more accessible, and impactful for people.
